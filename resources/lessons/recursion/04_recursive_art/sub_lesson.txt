# MS CS Long Block Lesson Plan

Time: 85 minutes

- Watch: "Fractals - The Hidden Dimension NOVA HD" on YouTube
    - Log into the desktop computer
        - username: csstudent
        - There is no password
    - https://youtu.be/d0Exnv8Ym7s
        - Video is 54:24 long
    - While watching, have them fill out the "Fractals, The Hidden Dimension" worksheet
        - Collect the worksheets at the end; just leave them on the table

- Give students seven minute break
    - Can do whatever they like as long as quiet and no violent games

- Give students ten minutes to thoughtfully fill out "Nice to Meet You" Questionnaires
    - Collect the questionnaires

- Last few minutes they can use either studying or quietly playing games
